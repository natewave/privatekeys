The Private Keys

Seven keys. One dead man's AI.

Nael Kadiri is twenty-four, a software developer in Paris, and newly orphaned — of an uncle he barely knew. Among Tarik's belongings: a leather notebook filled with coded pages, seven photographs of places he can't identify, and an encrypted USB drive. Inside the drive, locked behind a cipher only Nael can solve: an artificial intelligence trained on fourteen years of his uncle's inner life. The AI reveals that Tarik left behind a fortune locked in a Bitcoin wallet requiring seven keys, scattered across seven countries. To claim them, Nael must travel the world, solve the puzzles Tarik designed, and confront the family secrets buried alongside them.
